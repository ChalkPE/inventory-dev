Comme des Garcons : Coaches Jacket

  • Price$150
  • SizeL
  • ColorBlack

BUY

In 2011, Comme des Garcons opened its Good Design Shop in Tokyo in conjunction with Kenmei Nagaoka of D&Department Project, a store concept based on the principle of Long Life Design.

Offering various lifestyle and home goods, the Good Design Shop also hosts an in-house line of si mple and relatively affordable Comme des Garcons clothing based on archival designs. The Good Design Shop is now a permanent fixture with a more curated selection offered in Dover Street Market.

This classic coaches jacket is done in navy with a white back print of a repeated sporty graphic logo with segmented levels of halftone for a gradient effect and the classic CdG typeface logo below.
